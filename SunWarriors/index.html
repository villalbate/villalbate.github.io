"Sun Warriors is a free arcade multiplayer shooting game where you will control one of the four chosen ones by Naol's light. You will have to fight your way up the mountain and defeat numerous dark creatures sent by Aysel in order to destroy the portal and end the invasion."

Sun Warriors was developed during my final HND year by Angry Avocado, a small group of students, at ESAT Valencia. The game was developed in Unreal Engine 4 and it supports both local and online multiplayer. The game will be released on Steam in early 2021.
