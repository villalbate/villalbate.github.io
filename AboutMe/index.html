About Me

Pablo Villalba Teresa

I'm Pablo Villalba Teresa, a Videogames Programmer, from Valencia (Spain), currently studying Computer Science for Games at Sheffield Hallam University. Before studying at Sheffield I developed, as part of a team, Sun Warriors which will launch on Steam early 2021. Now, as part of my studies at Sheffield Hallam University I'm working on an augmented reality app that has photorealistic 3D rendering for my final year project.

During my studies at ESAT and Sheffield Hallam University I've gained experience developing for most of the current platforms. I'm always keen to learn new skills and to work with new technologies, that's why I'm working with AR for my final project as I'´ve always wanted to learn how to properly develop an AR app. I'm always looking forward to improve as a coder that's why I enjoy working as part of a team. I try to always work with enthusiasm, positivity and humor to keep the team motivated.

If you want to learn more about my projects you can check my portfolio or take a look at my resume
