Tower Smash

Tower Defense game developed in Unity3D for Android and iOS

Tower Smash is a free tower defense game where you have to protect your housee from the attack of different enemies. The game consists of 2 gamemodes, Story and Endless. In Story mode you go through different levels each one with a different theme, meanwhile, in Endless mode you have to survive the maximum number of rounds that you can in a single map.

Tower Smash was developed during my second HND year at ESAT Valencia. The game was developed in Unity3D for both Android and iOS.
